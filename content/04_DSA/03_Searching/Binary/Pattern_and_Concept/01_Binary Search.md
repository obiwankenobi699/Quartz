##  Binary Search Formula (for mid index)

To avoid integer overflow when computing mid:

$$
[

\text{mid} = \text{low} + \frac{\text{high} - \text{low}}{2}

]
$$

**Where:**

- `low` = starting index of search interval
- `high` = ending index of search interval
